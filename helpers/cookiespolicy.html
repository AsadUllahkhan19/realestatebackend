Cookies Policy

The Mac World Properties website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device that help us recognize you when you return to our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These essential cookies enable you to navigate our site and access secure areas.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies help us track visitor activity and usage patterns so we can enhance site functionality and performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to provide a more personalized experience, such as remembering your preferred language.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies record your browsing history to make our website and advertising more relevant to your interests. We may share this data with third-party advertising partners.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your web browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may impact your ability to use some personalized features on our site. Our use of cookies does not involve the collection of any personal information about you or your device usage.
